Nazar Veliasiuk
work

Nazar Veliasiuk (2003) is a designer and visual artist based in the Netherlands, currently studying at ArtEZ University of the Arts in Arnhem. His work spans editorial design, photography, moving image, and performance.

His work develops through an experimental approach, combining striking, saturated imagery with critical research. His visual language contrasts raw, confrontational imagery with sleek graphic aesthetics.

Recently, his practice has examined how identity and sexuality are constructed and performed through digital media. He investigates cultural topics such as corporate power structures, aesthetic norms, and sexual taboos.
